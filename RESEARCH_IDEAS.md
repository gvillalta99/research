## 📝 Notes & Observations

- **Observation**: Most ML papers cite 80-150 references; Systems papers cite 40-80
  - **Implication**: ML research might need larger corpus for adequate coverage

- **Observation**: Few papers cite cross-domain (ML + Systems + Data)
  - **Implication**: Opportunity for novel cross-disciplinary research

- **Observation**: Industry papers vastly underrepresented in academia
  - **Implication**: Need dedicated effort to find gray literature

- **Observation**: Pre-2015 foundational papers still heavily cited
  - **Implication**: Historical context crucial; don't ignore older research
